About Safari Travels

Rooted in Arusha, guiding travelers across Tanzania

Safari Travels is a Tanzania-based travel and tour company offering unforgettable safari adventures, mountain trekking, cultural experiences, and beach holidays.

Our Story

We are committed to providing exceptional service, professional guidance, and memorable experiences while showcasing the beauty and diversity of Tanzania. From the head office on Bondeni Street in Arusha, our team designs journeys through the Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Kilimanjaro and the islands of Zanzibar — built around each traveler's pace, budget, and sense of adventure.
