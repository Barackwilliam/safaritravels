Beyond the wildlife

Culture & Heritage

Tanzania is more than parks — it's over 120 tribes, centuries-old craft traditions, and flavours found nowhere else.

Meet the Maasai

Visit a living boma near Ngorongoro, share stories with elders, and learn the meaning behind the beadwork and the jump dance.

Tingatinga Art & Crafts

Watch painters at work in Arusha and Zanzibar studios, and take home hand-carved ebony, batik, and vivid Tingatinga canvases.

Zanzibar Spice & Cuisine

Walk a working spice farm, then sample nyama choma, ugali, and coconut fish curry the way locals actually eat them.

Music, Drums & Dance

Evenings around the fire with taarab melodies on the coast and ngoma drumming inland — an experience, not a show.
